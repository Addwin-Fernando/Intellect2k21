1. Photopedia:

    Show your skills, by capturing moments from today by creating memories for a lifetime.

  1. Only one entry is allowed per photographer.

  4. Only basic enhancements such as sharpening, contrast adjustment, or simple cropping will be allowed.
  5. Adult content of any kind would lead to direct disqualification.

    Let your Skill & Creativity speak out using your crafts.

  3. For every model a NAME TAG of the participant is mandatory.
  4. Sketches and highlighters can be used for your model.
  5. Any model (Picture) taken from the internet without the participants name tag will be disqualified.

  1. Any 2D and 3D model is allowed.
  2. Birds, animals, reptiles, aquatic animal models.
  3. DIY Flower baskets, Monumental models, Wall Hangers etc., and all other DIY models of 2d and 3d are allowed.
